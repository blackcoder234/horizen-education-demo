Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Find answers to our most frequently asked questions. If you don't see what you're looking for, please contact us.

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our office directly.

Can I schedule a campus tour before enrolling?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective students to visit our campus. You can schedule a tour through our contact form or by calling our admissions office.

How can I get technical support for the online learning platform?

For technical support, please email support@horizoneducation.com with details of the issue you're experiencing. Our tech team is available Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM EST.

Do you offer virtual meetings with instructors or advisors?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations with our instructors and academic advisors. You can request a meeting through the contact form, and we'll schedule a time that works for you.
